Aurora reimagines everyday banking around calm and confidence. The design system
leans on generous spacing, restrained color, and micro-interactions that make each
action feel deliberate.

## The challenge

Most banking apps overwhelm people at exactly the moment they want reassurance. We
set out to make the common tasks — checking a balance, moving money, understanding
a charge — feel quiet and certain.

## Approach

I led the end-to-end design and prototyped the core flows with subtle, physics-based
motion. Every transition carries meaning: money moves along a path, confirmations
settle into place, and nothing appears without a reason.

## Outcome

The redesign lifted task-completion rates and, more importantly, changed how people
described the app — from "stressful" to "steady."
