Field Notes needed an identity as rigorous as its research. The system pairs a
monospaced wordmark with an editorial serif, held together by a strict grid.

## System, not logo

Rather than a single mark, we built a kit of parts — type, grid, and a small set of
rules — that scales from business cards to conference stages while always feeling
like Field Notes.

## Details

The grid does the heavy lifting, giving the team confidence to lay out documents
themselves without breaking the brand.
