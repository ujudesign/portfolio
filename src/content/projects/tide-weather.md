Most weather apps overwhelm. Tide strips the experience down to a single, glanceable
view that adapts to the time of day and the conditions outside.

## Less, better

The interface hides everything but the essentials, letting the data speak through
gentle gradients and typography rather than dials and badges.

## Motion as information

Subtle transitions communicate change — a warming sky, an incoming front — so the
app feels alive without ever feeling busy.
